## Why Get Pre-Qualified?

- **Know Your Budget** — Understand exactly how much home you can afford
- **Strengthen Your Offer** — Sellers prefer pre-qualified buyers
- **Faster Closing** — Much of the paperwork is already done
- **No Obligation** — Pre-qualification is free and doesn't commit you to anything

**Note:** This is a pre-qualification request. Final approval requires a full application and documentation verification.
